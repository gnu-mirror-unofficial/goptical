void  set_tics_base(unsigned int min_count = 5, double base = 10.0 f, AxisMask a = XYZ)  

set distance between axis tics to best fit power of base divided by sufficient power of 2 to have at least min_count tics. Example: plot data range [0.01, 0.075] with base = 10 and min_count = 10 gives 13 tics intervals with a step distance of (10^-2)/(2^1) = 0.005
